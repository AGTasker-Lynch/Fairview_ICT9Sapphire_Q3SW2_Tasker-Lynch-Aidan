Left For Dead

One of the four playable Survivors in Left 4 Dead 2 is Nicolas, also known as Nick. He's a gambler with a dubious—even violent—history. He is hesitant to join the group at all and adopts the persona of "the complainer," criticizing the plans of others, the deteriorating condition of his once-fine suit, and the shortcomings of his fellow members. Because of this, he is initially hard for the others to get along with, but with time, as he gains their trust and establishes himself as an important member of the team, this changes.

In Left 4 Dead 2, Coach (actual name unknown) is one of the four playable Survivors. Before the Infection struck his hometown of Savannah, he was the coordinator for the freshman football team and a high school health instructor. Being the group's elder, he makes it his mission to inspire the other Survivors.

In Left 4 Dead, William "Bill" Overbeck is one of the four playable Survivors. He was a U.S. military veteran of the Vietnam War. The oldest member in the group and was in the Army 1st Special Forces Group. Typically, he is depicted brandishing the Assault weapon, which is actually the M16A2, an actual assault weapon that the US military employed following the Vietnam War. He frequently keeps a lit cigarette in his mouth, which goes out only when he passes away.
